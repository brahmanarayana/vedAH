<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
36. Verily both the fortune (*śrī́*) and the alliance (? *saṁvíd*) of  
the houses doth he etc. etc.

### Notes
The Pet. Lexx. interpret *saṁvíd* as 'possessions.'
