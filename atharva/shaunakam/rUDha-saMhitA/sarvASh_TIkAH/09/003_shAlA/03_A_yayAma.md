<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
3. He stretched (*ā-yam*), he combined (*sam-bṛh*), he made thy knots  
firm (*dṛḍhá*); with Indra we unfasten \[them\], as a knowing  
slaughterer the joints.
