<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
18. The cloud his fat, the conclusion (? *nidhána*) his marrow.

### Notes
In Ppp. this verse follows our 15, and there is added *bhūtyāṣ prajāyāṣ  
paśūnāṁ bhavati ya evaṁ veda*.
</details>
