<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
12. Hunger his paunch, cheer (*írā*) his rectum (? *vaniṣṭhú*), the  
mountains his *plāśís*.

### Notes
Ppp. reads at the end *prāśa*, for *plāśayaḥ*, and adds next vss. 16,  
17.
