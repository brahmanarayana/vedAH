### Translation
9. Both the Brahman-caste (*bráhman*) and the Kshatriya-caste (*kṣatrá*)  
his (two) hips, force his (two) thighs.

### Notes
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

Priestly rank and princely power are the hips, and strength is. the thigh.
