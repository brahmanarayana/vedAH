<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
11. Thought (*cétas*) his heart, wisdom his liver, ceremony (*vratá*)  
his *purītát*.

### Notes
The *pada*-text divides *puri॰tát*, but one can only wonder why. After  
*medhā*, Ppp. inserts *harimā cittam*.
</details>
