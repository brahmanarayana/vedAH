<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
6. My stone-defense art thou; whoever from the upward quarter etc. etc.

### Notes
It is possible to read these verses as 7 + 12 (or 13 ⌊or 14⌋): 5 = 24  
(or 25 ⌊or 26, vs. 2⌋).
</details>
