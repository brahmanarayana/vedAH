<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
6. This man of mine, O *kúṣṭha*—him bring, him relieve (*nis-kṛ*), him  
also make free from disease for me.

### Notes
With **c** compare the nearly identical vi. 95. 3 **d**. E.H. read *níḥ  
kuru*.
