<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
2. Smite down the sorcerers, smite down the witchcraft-maker; then,  
whoever seeks to injure us, him do thou smite, O herb.

### Notes
Ppp. omits, probably by oversight, the first half-verse.
