<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
5. The meters (*chándas*) at the sacrifice, O Maruts: hail! as a mother  
her son, fill ye here, joined.

### Notes
The meter and its treatment by the Anukr. are the same as in the case of  
vs. 1.
