<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
4. The directions (*prāiṣá*) at the sacrifice, the notices (*nivíd*):  
hail! taught by the wives, carry ye here, joined.

### Notes
Ppp. reads *prāiṣā nividā priyo yajūṅṣi śiṣṭāḥ* etc.
