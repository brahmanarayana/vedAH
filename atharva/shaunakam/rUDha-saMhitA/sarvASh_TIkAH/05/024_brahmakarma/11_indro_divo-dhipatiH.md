<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
11. Indra is overlord of heaven; let him favor etc. etc.

### Notes
In Ppp. (9) Indra is overlord of acts or rites (*kárman*); in TS. and  
śśS., of chiefs (*jyeṣṭhá*).
</details>
