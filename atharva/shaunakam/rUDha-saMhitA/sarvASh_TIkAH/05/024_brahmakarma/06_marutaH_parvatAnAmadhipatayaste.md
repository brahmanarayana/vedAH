### Translation
6. The Maruts are overlords of the mountains; let them favor etc. etc.

### Notes
According to Ppp. (11) and TS., Vishṇu is overlord of the mountains; TS.  
sets the Maruts over the troops (*gaṇa*), and śśS. gives them no place.
