### Translation
2. What wheedling (? *parirāpín*) man thou puttest forward, O niggard,  
to him of thine we pay homage: do not thou disturb my winning (*vaní*).

### Notes
The third pāda can be read as full only by violence. ⌊See *Gram.*  
§1048.⌋
