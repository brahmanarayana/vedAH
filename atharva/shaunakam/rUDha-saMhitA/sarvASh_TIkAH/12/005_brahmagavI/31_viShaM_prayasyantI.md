### Translation
31. Poison when heating (? *pra-yas*), *takmán* when heated.

### Notes
All the mss. read *práyastā*, but Bp. has *pra॰yáṅchaṅtī*, Bs.  
*-yachantī*, emended to *-yasy-*, P.M.W. *-yásyaṅchaṅtī* (M. emended to  
*-yasy-* ⌊?⌋).
