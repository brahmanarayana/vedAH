<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
2. He bargains away his progeny and becomes exhausted of cattle who is  
not willing to give the cow (*gó*) of the gods to the sons of seers that  
ask for her.

### Notes
⌊Pādas **c, d** recur as 12 **a, b**.⌋
