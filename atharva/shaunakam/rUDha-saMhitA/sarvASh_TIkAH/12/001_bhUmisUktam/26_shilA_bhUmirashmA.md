### Translation
26. Rock \[is?\] earth (*bhū́mi*), stone, dust; this earth (*bhū́mi*)  
\[is\] held together, held; to that earth, gold-backed (*-vákṣas*) have  
I paid homage.

### Notes
Ppp. reads, in **a-b**, *pāṅsv aryā bhūmi stṛtā dhṛtā*, and omits **c,  
d**. ⌊Cf. note to vs. 6.⌋
</details>
