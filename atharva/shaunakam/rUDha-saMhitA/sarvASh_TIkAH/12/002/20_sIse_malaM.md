<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
20. Having settled what is foul upon the lead \[and\] headache upon the  
pillow, having wiped off on the black ewe, be ye cleansed, fit for  
sacrifice.

### Notes
Compare xiv. 2. 67. ⌊Cf. MGS. ii. 1. 10.⌋
