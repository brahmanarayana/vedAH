<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
46. Overcoming, O Agni, all \[our\] rivals, do thou assign to us their  
refreshment \[and\] wealth.

### Notes
The Anukr. agrees with the mss. in reckoning these two *triṣṭubh* pādas  
as a whole verse.
</details>
