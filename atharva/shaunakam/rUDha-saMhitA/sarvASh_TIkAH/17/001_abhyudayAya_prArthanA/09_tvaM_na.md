<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
9. Do thou, O Indra, in order to great good-fortune, protect us about  
with unharmed rays.—Thine, O Vishṇu etc. etc.

### Notes
The comm. takes *aktúbhis* in its sense of 'night.' Ppp. reads  
*adabdhāiṣ pari* in **b**.
</details>
