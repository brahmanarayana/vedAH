### Translation
20. From thy mouth came (*bhū*) the songs (*gā́thā*), from thy  
nape-bones, O cow, \[came\] force; from thy belly (? *pājasyà*) was born  
the sacrifice, from thy teats the rays.

### Notes
Ppp. reads in **a** *bhavanti* for *abhavan*.
