<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
12. In three vessels the heavenly cow took that soma, where Atharvan,  
consecrated, sat on a golden *barhís*.

### Notes
Ppp. reads *hitaṁ* for *tam* in **a**, and *ādyevy abharad* in **b**.
