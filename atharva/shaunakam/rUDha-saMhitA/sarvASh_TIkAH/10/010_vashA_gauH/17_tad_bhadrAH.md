### Translation
17. There the excellent ones united, the cow, the directress, also the  
*svadhā́*, where Atharvan, consecrated, sat on a golden *barhís*.

### Notes
The second half-verse is identical with 12 **c, d** above. Ppp. reads in  
**a** *gachanti*.
