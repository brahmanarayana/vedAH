<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
20. What neck-bones (*grīvā́*) are thine, what shoulder-bones  
⌊*skandhá*⌋, what side-bones (*pṛṣṭí*), and what ribs (*párśu*)—let them  
yield etc. etc.

### Notes
The Anukr. does not notice the lacking syllable in **a**.
</details>
