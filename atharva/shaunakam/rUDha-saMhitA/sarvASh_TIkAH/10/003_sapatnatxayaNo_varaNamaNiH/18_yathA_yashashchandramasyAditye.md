### Translation
18. As glory \[is\] in the moon, and in the men-beholding Āditya, so let  
the *varaṇá* amulet etc. etc.

### Notes
From here on, Ppp. has the same refrain ⌊as the Berlin text⌋, only  
reading at the end *mām*.
