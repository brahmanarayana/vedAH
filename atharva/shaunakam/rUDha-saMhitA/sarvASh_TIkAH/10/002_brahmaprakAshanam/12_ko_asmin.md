### Translation
12. Who set form in him? who both bulk (*mahmán*) and name? who \[set\]  
in him progress (*gātú*)? who display (*ketú*)? who \[set\] behaviors  
(*carítra*) in man?

### Notes
Ppp. again *pāuruṣe* at the end.
