<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
24. By whom is this earth disposed? by whom the sky set above? by whom  
this atmosphere, the expanse, set aloft and across?

### Notes
Ppp. reads, for **a**, *kene ’daṁ bhūmir nihataḥ*.
</details>
