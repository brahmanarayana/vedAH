### Translation
19. Of whom they call *bráhman* the mouth, the honey-whip the tongue  
also, of whom they call *virā́j* the udder—that Skambha etc. etc.

### Notes
Ppp. reads for **c** *virājaṁ yasyo ”dhā ”hus*.
