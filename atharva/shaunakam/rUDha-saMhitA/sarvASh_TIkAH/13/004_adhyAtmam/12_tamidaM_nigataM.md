<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
12. Into him is entered (*ni-gam*) this power; he himself is one,  
single (*ekavṛ́t*), one only.

### Notes
The verse lacks four syllables of the *gāyatrī* number, instead of two,  
as the Anukr. counts.
</details>
