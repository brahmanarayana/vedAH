### Translation
13. These gods in him become single.

### Notes
The Anukr. counts fourteen syllables in the verse; one does not see  
where it finds more than thirteen.

16, 19. prājāpatyā ’nuṣṭubh; 17, 18. āsurī gāyatrī.*\]
