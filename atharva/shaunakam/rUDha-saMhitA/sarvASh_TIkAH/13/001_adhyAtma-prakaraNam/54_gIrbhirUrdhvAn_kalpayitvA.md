<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
54. Having shaped \[them\] aloft by songs, the ruddy one said to the  
earth: in thee let this all be born, what is (*bhūtá*) or what is to be.

### Notes
Ppp. reads at the end *bhavyam*.
