### Translation
50. The one is all set in truth, the other is kindled in the waters:  
kindled with etc. etc.

### Notes
Ppp. reads in **b** *samāhitas* ⌊again: cf. 48⌋ for *sam idhyate*, and  
adds another pāda: *satye adbhis samāhitaḥ*.
