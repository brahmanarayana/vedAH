### Translation
21. With whom \[as\] eye thou, O purifying Varuṇa, seest him that busies  
himself among the people (*jánān*).

### Notes
SV. (i. 638) reads at the beginning *yéna;* VS. (xxxiii. 32) agrees with  
RV. and our text.
