### Translation
5. ⌊13.⌋ For him, from the fixed quarter, ⌊14.⌋ they made the two winter  
months guardians, and earth and fire attendants. ⌊15.⌋ The two winter  
months guard from the fixed quarter, and earth and fire attend, him who  
knoweth thus.
