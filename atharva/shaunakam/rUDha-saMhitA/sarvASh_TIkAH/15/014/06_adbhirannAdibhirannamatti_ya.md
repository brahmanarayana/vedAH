<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
6. ⌊11.⌋ As he moved out toward the cattle, Rudra, coming into being,  
moved out after, making the herbs \[his\] food-eaters; ⌊12.⌋ with the  
herbs as food-eaters doth he eat food who knoweth thus.

### Notes
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

He, when he went away to animals, went away having become Rudra and having made herbs eaters of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with herbs as food-eaters.
</details>
