<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
2. ⌊3.⌋ As he moved out toward the southern quarter, Indra, coming into  
being, moved out after, making strength \[his\] food-eater; ⌊4.⌋ with  
strength as food-eater doth he eat food who knoweth thus.

### Notes
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

He, when he went away to the southern region, went away having become Indra, and having made Strength an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with strength as food-eater.
</details>

<details open><summary>पण्डित क्षेमकरणदास त्रिवेदी - पदार्थः</summary>

पदार्थान्वयभाषाः -  (अन्नादेन) जीवनरक्षक (मनसा) मन के साथ वह [अतिथि] (अन्नम्) जीवन की (अत्ति) रक्षा करता है, (यः) जो (एवम्) व्यापक परमात्मा को (वेद) जानता है ॥२॥
</details>

<details open><summary>पण्डित क्षेमकरणदास त्रिवेदी - भावार्थः</summary>

भावार्थभाषाः -  ब्रह्मज्ञानी विद्वान्अतिथि अपने लगातार सदुपदेशों सत्कर्मों और सत्पराक्रमों से लोगों को बलवान् करकेसंसार की रक्षा करता है ॥१, २॥
</details>
