<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
2. ⌊3.⌋ As he moved out toward the southern quarter, Indra, coming into  
being, moved out after, making strength \[his\] food-eater; ⌊4.⌋ with  
strength as food-eater doth he eat food who knoweth thus.

### Notes
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

He, when he went away to the southern region, went away having become Indra, and having made Strength an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with strength as food-eater.
