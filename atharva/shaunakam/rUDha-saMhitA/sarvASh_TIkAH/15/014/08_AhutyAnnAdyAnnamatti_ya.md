### Translation
8.⌊15.⌋ As he moved out toward men (*manuṣyà*), Agni, coming into being,  
moved out after, making the cry *svā́hā* ('hail') \[his\] food-eater;  
⌊16.⌋ with the cry *svā́hā*as food-eater doth he eat food who knoweth  
thus.

### Notes
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

He, when he went away to men, went away having become Agni and having made the exclamation Svaha an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with the exclamation Svaha as food-eater.
