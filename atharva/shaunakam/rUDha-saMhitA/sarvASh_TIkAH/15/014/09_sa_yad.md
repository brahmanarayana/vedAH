<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
9. ⌊17.⌋ As he moved out toward the upward quarter, Brihaspati, coming  
into being, moved out after, making the cry *váṣaṭ* \[his\] food-eater;  
⌊18.⌋ with the cry *váṣaṭ* as food-eater doth he eat food who knoweth  
thus.

### Notes
The first pāda is not metrically defined by the Anukr.
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

He, when he went away to the upper region, went away having become Brihaspati and having made the exclamation Vashat an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with the exclamation Vashat as food-eater.
</details>
