<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
2. He arose toward (*abhi॰*) the tribes (*víś*), the kinsmen, food,  
food-eating.

### Notes
Half the mss. (Bp.Bs.p.m.E.O.D.K.) omit *ánnam;* the metrical definition  
of the Anukr. implies its presence.
</details>
