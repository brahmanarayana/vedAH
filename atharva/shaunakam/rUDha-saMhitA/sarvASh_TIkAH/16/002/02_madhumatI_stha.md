### Translation
2. Rich in honey are ye; may I speak speech rich in honey.

### Notes
'Ye': i.e., the waters, the adjective being feminine. ⌊We had a phrase  
like to our second clause at iii. 20. 10: cf. *Gram.* §738 a.⌋
