<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
4. Whom we hate, and who hates us, to him we make it go.

### Notes
Our *yás* (in *yáś ca no*) is an emendation for *yát*, which all the  
mss. read. ⌊SPP. reads *yát* with all his authorities.⌋
</details>
