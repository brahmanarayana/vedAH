<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
9. Waking evil-dreaming, sleeping evil-dreaming.

### Notes
Literally 'of one waking' and 'in sleep.' The *pada*-text reads  
*॰dusvapnyám* both times. The Anukr. twice resolves *-ni-am*.
