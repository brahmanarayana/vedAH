<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
1. May I be the navel of riches, the navel of my equals.

### Notes
The scholiast (*pariśiṣṭa*) adds this verse (or *paryāya*) 3. 1 under  
Kāuś. 18. 25. ⌊Cf. note to 3. 1.⌋
