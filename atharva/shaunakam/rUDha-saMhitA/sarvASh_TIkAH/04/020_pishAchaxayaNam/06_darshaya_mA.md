### Translation
6. Show me the sorcerers; show the sorceresses; show all the *piśācás:*  
with this intent I take hold of (*ā-rabh*) thee, O herb.

### Notes
For second half-verse, Ppp. has *āpaspṛg eva tiṣṭhantaṁ darśaya māṁ  
kimīdinam*.
