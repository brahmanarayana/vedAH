### Translation
1. By thee of old the Atharvans slew the demons, O herb; by thee did  
Kaśyapa slay; by thee Kaṇva, Agastya.

### Notes
The comm. explains that one or other of the specified plants, the  
*sahamānā* etc., is here addressed.
</details>
