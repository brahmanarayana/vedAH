### Translation
8. Let Soma favor me with what is Soma's (*sāúmya*), in order to breath  
etc. etc.

### Notes
The comm. explains *sāúmyena* as *somatvasampādakena dharmeṇa  
jagadāpyāyanakāritvādidharmeṇa*.
</details>
