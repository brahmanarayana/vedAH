### Translation
3. To them of six verses, hail!

### Notes
All the *saṁhitā*-mss. read *ṣaḍarc-*, and two of SW's *pada*-mss.  
*ṣaḍá॰ṛc-*; both editions *ṣaḍṛc-*, with the comm. and three *pada*-mss.  
The Gop.Br. has *ṣaḍarc-* in i. 1. 5.
