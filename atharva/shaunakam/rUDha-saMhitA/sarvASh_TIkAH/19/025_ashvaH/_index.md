### Comment
The verse is not found in Pāipp. The comm. finds it quoted in Nakṣ. K. 17-18, in a mahāśānti ceremony called gāndharvī, on occasion of the loss (kṣaya) of a horse.

Translated: Griffith, ii. 282.
