### Translation
16. *Bṛhát* the stirring-stick, *rathaṁtará* the spoon.

### Notes
The comm. paraphrases *āyavana* with *udake prakṣiptānāṁ taṇḍulānām  
miśraṇasādhanaṁ kāṣṭham*.
</details>
