<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
11. This very earth is the vessel (*kumbhī́*) of the finishing (*rādh*)  
rice-dish, heaven the cover.

### Notes
⌊W. interlines a query over "finishing." The comm. renders *rādh-* by  
*pacyamānasya*.⌋
