<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
1. Of this rice-dish Brihaspati is the head, Brahman the mouth  
(*múkha*).

### Notes
The comm. combines in part two or three verses of the first *paryāya*  
together in giving his explanations.
</details>
