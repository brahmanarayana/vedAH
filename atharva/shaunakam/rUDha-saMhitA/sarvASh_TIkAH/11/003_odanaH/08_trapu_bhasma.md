<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
8. Tin \[its\] ash, greens (? *háritam*) \[its\] color, blue lotus  
(*púṣkara*) its smell.

### Notes
The comm. glosses *hárita* with *heman* 'gold.' ⌊Over "greens" W. has  
interlined "gold? (so BR.)." He rendered *hárita* by "the yellow one" at  
v. 28. 5, 9.⌋
</details>
