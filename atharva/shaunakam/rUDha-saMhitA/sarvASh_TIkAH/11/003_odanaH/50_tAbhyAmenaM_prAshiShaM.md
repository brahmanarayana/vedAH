<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
50. This—namely, the rice-dish—is indeed the summit (*viṣṭápa*) of the  
ruddy one (*bradhná*).

### Notes
The comm. explains *bradhna* as *sūryamaṇḍalamadhyavartī ”śvaraḥ*, and  
*viṣṭapa* as *viyati viṣṭabdham maṇḍalam*.
