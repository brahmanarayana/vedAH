<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
10. Entrails \[its\] 'neck-ropes (? *jatrú*), intestines \[its\] straps.

### Notes
*Jatrú* is rendered with the commentary, who explains it as  
*anaḍudgrīvāṇāṁ śakaṭayojanārthā rajjavaḥ*.
