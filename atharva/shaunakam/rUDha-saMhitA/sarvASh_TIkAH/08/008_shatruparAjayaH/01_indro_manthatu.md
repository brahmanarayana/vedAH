<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
1. Let Indra the shaker shake (*math*) \[them\], he the mighty hero,  
stronghold-splitter, in order that we may slay by thousands the armies  
of our enemies.

### Notes
Quoted in Kāuś. 16.9 ⌊see introd.⌋.
