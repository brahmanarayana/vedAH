### Translation
4. Forth out of *bṛhát* \[as\] sixth five sāmans \[were\] fashioned;  
*bṛhát* was fashioned out of *bṛhatī́;* out of what was *bṛhatī́* made?

### Notes
Ppp. reads *ṣaṣṭhaḥ* instead of -*ṭhāt*.
</details>
