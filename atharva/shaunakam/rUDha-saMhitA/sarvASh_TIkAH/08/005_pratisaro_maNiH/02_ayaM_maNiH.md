<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
2. This amulet, rival-slaying, having excellent heroes, powerful,  
vigorous, overpowering, formidable, a hero, goes to meet the  
witchcrafts, spoiling \[them\].

### Notes
Ppp. reads *etu* in **c**.
