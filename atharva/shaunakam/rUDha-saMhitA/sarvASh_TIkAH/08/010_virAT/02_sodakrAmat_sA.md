### Translation
2. She ascended (*ut-kram*); she descended (*ni-kram*) in the  
householder's fire (*gā́rhapatya*); house-sacrificing (*gṛhamedhín*)  
house-holder ⌊*gṛhápati*⌋ becometh he who knoweth thus.
