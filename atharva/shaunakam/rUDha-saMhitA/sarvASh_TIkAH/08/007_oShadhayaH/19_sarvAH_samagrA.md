<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
19. Let all the entire herbs note (*budh*) my spell (*vácas*), that we  
may make this man pass forth out of difficulty.

### Notes
Ppp. omits the second half-verse; it is identical with 7 **c, d**,  
above.
