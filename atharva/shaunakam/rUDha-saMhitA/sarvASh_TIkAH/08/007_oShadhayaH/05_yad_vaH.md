### Translation
5. What power \[is\] yours, ye powerful ones, \[what\] heroism and what  
strength \[is\] yours, therewith, O herbs, free ye this man from this  
*yákṣma;* now (*átho*) do I make a remedy.

### Notes
The last pāda is wanting in Ppp.
