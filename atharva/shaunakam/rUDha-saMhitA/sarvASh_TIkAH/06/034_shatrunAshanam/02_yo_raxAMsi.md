### Translation
2. He who burns down the demons, Agni, with sharp heat (*śocís*): may  
he etc. etc.

### Notes
RV. has *vṛ́ṣā śukréṇa* at beginning of **b**.
</details>

That Agni who with sharpened flame of fire consumes the Rakshasas, So may he bear us past our foes.
