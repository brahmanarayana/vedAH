<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
1. As both the heaven and the earth do not fear, are not harmed, so, my  
breath, fear not.

### Notes
⌊MGS., at i. 2. 13, has *evam me prāṇa mā bibha evam me prāṇa mā  
riṣaḥ.*⌋
