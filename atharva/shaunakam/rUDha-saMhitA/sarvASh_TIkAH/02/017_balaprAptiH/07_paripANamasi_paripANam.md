### Translation
7. Protection (*paripā́ṇa*) art thou; protection mayest thou give me:  
hail!

### Notes
The anuvāka ⌊3.⌋ has 7 hymns, with 42 verses; the Anukr. says: *aṣṭonaṁ  
tasmāc chatārdhaṁ tṛtīye.*  
  
Here ends also the third *prapāṭhaka.*
</details>
