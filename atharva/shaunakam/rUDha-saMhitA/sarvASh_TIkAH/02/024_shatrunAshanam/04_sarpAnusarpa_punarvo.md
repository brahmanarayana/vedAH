<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
4. O *sarpá, anusarpa!* back again let your familiar etc. etc.

### Notes
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

O Sarpa, Anusarpa, back return your arts of witchery! Back, 'i Kimidins! let your weapon fall, etc.
</details>

<details open><summary>पदपाठः</summary>

सर्प। अनुऽसर्प। पुनः। वः। यन्तु। यातवः। पुनः। हेतिः। किमीदिनः। यस्य। स्थ। तम्। अत्त। यः। वः। प्रऽअहैत्। तम्। अत्त। स्वा। मांसानि। अत्त। २४.४।
</details>
