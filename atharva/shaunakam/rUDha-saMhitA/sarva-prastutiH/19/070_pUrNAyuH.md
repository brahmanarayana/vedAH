#### Translation
1. Live, O Indra; live, O Sūrya; live ye, O gods; may I live; may I live  
my whole life-time.

#### Notes
The mss. reads *jī́vās* after *dévās*, and SPP. retains it, false accent  
and all. The comm. explains it by adding a *bhavata*.

</details>
