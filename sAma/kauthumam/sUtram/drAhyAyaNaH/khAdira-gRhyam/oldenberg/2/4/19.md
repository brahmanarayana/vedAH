19. Having directed him (to observe the duties of Brahmaarya, by the formula), 'A student art thou' (l.l. 25, 26), (the teacher) sitting down should from left to right tie round the student, who bends his right knee and clasps his hands, the girdle made of Mua grass, and should cause him to repeat (the verse), 'Protecting us from evil word' (l.l. 27).
