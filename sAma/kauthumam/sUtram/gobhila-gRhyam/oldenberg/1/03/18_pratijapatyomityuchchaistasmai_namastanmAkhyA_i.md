18. [^11]  He replies in a loud voice, 'Om!' Then in a low voice: 'To that (food) I bring adoration. May it not fail!'


[^11]:  The MSS. give mākhyā and mākṣā. We ought to read, tan ma kṣāyīty upāṃśu. Comp. Āpastamba II, 2, 3, 11
