3. [^3] Should, with the permission (of his parents), take a wife,

[^3]: I prefer to supply, (with the permission) of his parents, and not, of his teacher. Hiraṇyakesin says, samāvṛtta ākāryakulāt mātāpitarau bibhṛyāt, tābhyām anugñāto bhāryām upayakkḥet.
